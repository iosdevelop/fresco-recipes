Vegetable Soup Powder From Wincos Bulk Section Pepperpot Soup

A vegan pepperpot soup built around vegetable soup powder from Wincos bulk section with appliance-friendly steps and Jamaican-inspired island seasoning.

Prep 15 min · Cook 40 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium tamari or soy sauce, optional
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable soup powder from Wincos bulk section
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup diced carrots

Instructions

  1. Steam potatoes and cauliflower: pour 1 cup water into the Instant Pot inner pot. Place the vegetables on a rack, seal, and Steam on High for 3 minutes until fork-tender; quick-release, remove the vegetables, and discard the water.
  2. Select Saute on High and cook the onion, garlic, scallions, thyme, and allspice in the oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the main ingredients, coconut milk, tamari, and 1/2 cup water. Scrape the inner pot clean, then seal the lid.
  4. Pressure Cook on High for 5 minutes, then allow a 5-minute natural release before venting the remaining pressure.
  5. Select Saute on Low for 3 to 5 minutes if the sauce needs thickening, then finish with lime juice and fresh herbs.
