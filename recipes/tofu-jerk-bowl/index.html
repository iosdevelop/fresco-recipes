Tofu Jerk Bowl

A vegan jerk bowl built around tofu with appliance-friendly steps and Jamaican-inspired island seasoning.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Email recipeOpen recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, divided
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium tamari or soy sauce, optional
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 Scotch bonnet pepper, minced, optional
  • 1 cup tofu
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves

Instructions

  1. Steam cauliflower: add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, then set florets on a rack until crisp-tender before folding them into the dish.
  2. Coat vegetables or beans with garlic, thyme, allspice, ginger, and Scotch bonnet.
  3. Roast or air fry until lightly charred.
  4. Serve over brown rice with lime, scallions, and a small spoon of coconut sauce.
