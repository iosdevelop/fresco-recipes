Savory Mushroom Patties

Savory vegan patties built around mushrooms with Jamaican-inspired island seasoning and complete appliance instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 35 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium tamari or soy sauce, optional
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 cup oat flour or whole wheat flour, as needed
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 14 ounces extra-firm tofu, pressed and crumbled

Instructions

  1. Preheat the air fryer on Air Fry at 375F for 3 minutes.
  2. Finely chop the main ingredients and mix them with scallions, thyme, allspice, tamari, lime juice, and enough oat flour to hold together.
  3. Shape into 8 patties about 1/2 inch thick and lightly brush or spray both sides with oil.
  4. Air Fry at 375F for 14 minutes, flipping after 7 minutes, until browned, crisp at the edges, and firm in the center.
  5. Rest for 3 minutes before serving with lime and fresh herbs.
