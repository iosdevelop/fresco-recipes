Coconut Brown Rice

A vegan Jamaican staple built for weeknight cooking and appliance-friendly instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Email recipeOpen recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions

  1. Rinse brown rice.
  2. Cook with coconut milk, water, thyme, garlic, and scallions.
  3. Keep warm for ten minutes before serving.
  4. Finish with lime juice and fresh herbs to brighten the flavor.
