Jamaican Potatoes Coconut Bowl

A vegan Jamaican recipe built around available ingredients and appliance-friendly cooking.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • Tamari or soy sauce, to taste
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves

Instructions

  1. Steam potatoes: add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, then set potatoes on a rack until just tender before adding them to the main preparation.
  2. Steam cauliflower: add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, then set florets on a rack until crisp-tender before folding them into the dish.
  3. Saute onion, garlic, scallions, thyme, and allspice until fragrant.
  4. Add the remaining vegetables, coconut milk, and a splash of tamari or soy sauce.
  5. Simmer until glossy, then adjust salt, heat, and lime to taste.
  6. Hold on Keep Warm for service and serve with brown rice or dumplings.
