Chickpea Curry Bowl

A vegan Jamaican staple built for weeknight cooking and appliance-friendly instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chickpeas
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon Jamaican curry powder
  • 2 scallions, sliced

Instructions

  1. Steam potatoes and cauliflower first.
  2. Saute aromatics with curry powder.
  3. Simmer chickpeas, vegetables, and coconut milk until glossy.
