Jamaican Potatoes Patties

A vegan Jamaican patties built around potatoes with appliance-friendly steps and bright island seasoning.

Prep 15 min · Cook 35 min · Serves 4

Email recipeOpen recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, divided
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium tamari or soy sauce, optional
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 cup oat flour or whole wheat flour, as needed
  • 2 cups diced potatoes
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chickpeas

Instructions

  1. Steam potatoes: add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, then set potatoes on a rack until just tender before adding them to the main preparation.
  2. Steam cauliflower: add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, then set florets on a rack until crisp-tender before folding them into the dish.
  3. Mash the filling with scallions, thyme, allspice, and a splash of coconut milk.
  4. Spoon into vegan pastry rounds or roasted vegetable boats.
  5. Bake until the filling is hot and the edges are crisp.
