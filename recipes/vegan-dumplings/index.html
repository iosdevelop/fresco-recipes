Vegan Dumplings

A vegan Jamaican staple built for weeknight cooking and appliance-friendly instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup water, plus 1 to 2 tablespoons more as needed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions

  1. Mix dough until soft.
  2. Shape small dumplings.
  3. Add 1 cup water to the Instant Pot inner pot, set dumplings on a steamer rack, and steam until cooked through; or simmer in stew until tender.
  4. Finish with lime juice and fresh herbs to brighten the flavor.
