Vegan Mushroom Gravy

A vegan Jamaican staple built for weeknight cooking and appliance-friendly instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Email recipeOpen recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon tamari
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice

Instructions

  1. Saute mushrooms until browned.
  2. Add onion, garlic, thyme, tamari, and stock.
  3. Simmer until thick enough to coat a spoon.
  4. Finish with lime juice and fresh herbs to brighten the flavor.
