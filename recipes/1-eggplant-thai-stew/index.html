1 Eggplant Thai Stew

A savory vegan Thai-inspired stew with layered aromatics and a bright finish.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 potatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic, scallions, thyme, and allspice until fragrant.
  2. Add 1 eggplant, 1 potatoes, 1 red bell pepper, 1/2 cup coconut milk, and 1 cup water. Sauté until potatoes are fork-tender.
  3. Finish with fresh lime juice and herbs; adjust heat to taste.
