Chickpeas Jamaican Stew

A savory vegan Jamaican-inspired stew with layered aromatics and a bright lime finish.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • sweet potatoes
  • scallions

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic, scallions, thyme, and allspice until fragrant.
  2. Add the prepared ingredients and 1 cup water, then Pressure Cook until tender and cohesive.
  3. Finish with fresh lime juice and herbs; adjust heat to taste.
