Jamaican Rice and Peas

A vegan Jamaican staple built for weeknight cooking and appliance-friendly instructions.

Prep 15 min · Cook 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked kidney beans
  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook soaked kidney beans until tender.
  2. Cook brown rice with coconut milk, thyme, scallions, and beans.
  3. Rest covered before fluffing.
